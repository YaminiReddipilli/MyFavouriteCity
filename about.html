About Visakhapatnam

Visakhapatnam, also known as Vizag, is a port city and the financial capital of Andhra Pradesh, India. Located along the eastern coast of India, it is renowned for its scenic beaches, rich culture, and historical landmarks. Vizag serves as an important industrial and educational hub with a thriving economy.

Key Highlights

  • Beautiful beaches like RK Beach and Rushikonda Beach
  • Rich cultural heritage and historical landmarks
  • One of the major ports of India
  • Home to India's Eastern Naval Command
